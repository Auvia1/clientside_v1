hospital landing pageAuvia Labs Landing Page (AI Healthcare SaaS)

You are a senior SaaS product designer designing a modern startup landing page in Figma.

Design a high-end SaaS landing page for a healthcare AI startup called "Auvia Labs."

Auvia Labs provides AI receptionist software for hospitals and clinics that automatically handles:

• incoming patient calls
• appointment booking
• call analytics
• clinic automation

The product includes two dashboards:

• Admin Portal (for managing multiple clinics)
• Clinic Portal (for clinic staff)

The landing page should communicate the product clearly and visually, showing how clinics use the platform.
